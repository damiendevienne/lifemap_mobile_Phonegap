The Tree of Life

Navigation (1/6)

In Lifemap, exploration is done by zooming and paning, just like in geographic maps.

Tap and hold finger on screen to pan.
Pinch two fingers to zoom in and out.

Searching (2/6)

Click in the search bar on the top to find species, genera, families, etc. Suggestions appear while you are typing.


Click on your choice to focus on it.

Interactive nodes (3/6)

All nodes and tips in the tree are interactive. When clicking on a node, you have access to a picture and a descritpion that is retrieved from Wikipedia.

Additional information (4/6)

From the Wikipedia description, you can access direcly wikipedia if you want more details or if you want to edit or create the content of the page.

The "View full ancestry" button gives the route from the current node to the root of the tree (see next page).

Routes (5/6)

Routes can also be computed from any two species or nodes in the tree. To do this, click on the icon at the bottom right corner of the screen and give the name of the source and destination clades/taxa.

Routes (6/6)

When source and destination have been entered, the "route" is highlighted and an new bar appears at the bottom.

The icon at the bottom right corner details all clades encountered when going from the source to the destination. This list is interactive.
